## Privacy Principles
Microsoft’s approach to privacy is based on the following principles:

- **Control**
- **Transparency**
- **Security**
- **Strong legal protections**
- **No content-based targeting**
- **Benefits to you**
  - Troubleshooting
  - Feature improvement
  - Personalized customer experience

---

## Microsoft Priva

### Priva Privacy Risk Management
Provides visibility into organizational data and policy templates to reduce privacy risks.

- Detects overexposed personal data so users can secure it
- Spots and limits transfers of personal data across departments or regional borders
- Helps identify and reduce unused personal data stored by the organization

### Priva Subject Rights Requests
- Automates and manages workflows for fulfilling data subject requests

## Key Notes
