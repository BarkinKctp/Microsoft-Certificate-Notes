# Azure Policy and Compliance

- Data Catalog – enables data discovery  
- Data Sharing – shares data within and between organizations  
- Data Estate Insights – accesses data estate health  
- Data Policy – governs access to data  

---

## Governance Concepts

- Resource lock prevents resources from being accidentally deleted or changed  
- Resource tags offer custom grouping of resources  
- Policies enforce rules across all resource configurations to keep them compliant with corporate standards  
- Initiative is a way of grouping related policies together  

---

## Azure Policy

Azure Policy is a service in Azure that enables you to create, assign, and manage policies that control or audit resources.  
These policies enforce different rules across all resource configurations so that the configurations stay compliant with corporate standards.
